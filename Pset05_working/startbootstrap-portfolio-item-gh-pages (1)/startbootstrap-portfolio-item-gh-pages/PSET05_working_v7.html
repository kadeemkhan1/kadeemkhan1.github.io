Transport and Vulnerability The Effects of the Increased Intensity of Storms on the City of Boston

Project Summary

Studies show that climate change is leading to the increased intensity of storms and temperatures around the world. In the last decade, the Northeast region of the United States has experienced a rise in the number of and intensity of storms. Our project is aimed at visualizing and describing the effects of extreme weather events on the Boston metro region. Furthermore, we acknowledge the fact that socio-economically vulnerable populations are most affected by delays and closures. Hence, our group aims to highlight the effects of extreme weather conditions on the transport system and on vulnerable populations using compelling data visuals.
